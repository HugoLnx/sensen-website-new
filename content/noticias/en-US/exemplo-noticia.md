## What's New?

We're excited to announce the **biggest Typomancer update ever**! This version brings brand new content, quality of life improvements, and important fixes.

### New Spells

- **Meteor Shower**: An epic spell that deals area damage
- **Time Warp**: Distorts time to gain advantage in battles
- **Shadow Clone**: Creates clones of your words for double attacks

### Gameplay Improvements

Typing speed now directly influences damage dealt. The faster you type correctly, the more powerful your character becomes!

> "The new combo mechanic completely transformed the game experience." — *Community Review*

## How to Access

To update your game, simply visit the [Typomancer page on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com) and download the update automatically.

We thank all players who sent feedback and helped us make Typomancer even better!
