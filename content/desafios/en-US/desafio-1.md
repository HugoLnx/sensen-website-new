# Challenge 1: Typing Master

TyWorld is under attack and only the fastest can save it. Your goal is to complete all stages of Typomancer with S-Rank.

## Requirements
- Complete all stages.
- Make no more than 5 mistakes per stage.
- Maintain an accuracy above 95%.

## Rewards
By completing this challenge, you will unlock the exclusive **Light Speed** title in your global profile.
