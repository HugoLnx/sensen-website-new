## Introduction

**Typomancer** is a typing RPG where your speed and accuracy define your character's power. This guide covers everything you need to know to get started.

## Basic Mechanics

### Typing System

- Type the words that appear on screen as fast as possible
- Longer words deal more damage
- Mistakes reduce your combo multiplier

### Starting Spells

1. **Fireball** — Type "fire" to cast
2. **Ice Shard** — Type "ice" to freeze enemies
3. **Heal** — Type "heal" to recover health

> **Tip:** Practice in Training Mode before facing bosses!

## Progression

| Level | Unlock | Minimum Speed |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Fireball | 20 WPM |
| 5 | Ice Shard | 35 WPM |
| 10 | Thunder | 50 WPM |
| 15 | Meteor | 70 WPM |

## Advanced Controls

Use **keyboard shortcuts** to accelerate your gameplay:

- `Tab` — Selects the next enemy
- `Space` — Skips dialogues
- `Esc` — Pauses the game

## Conclusion

With consistent practice, you'll master Typomancer in no time. Good luck in the dungeons!
